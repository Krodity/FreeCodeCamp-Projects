Grandma's Chocolate Chip Cookies

These classic cookies are chewy, rich, and packed with gooey chocolate chips. Perfect for a cozy afternoon treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Gradually mix in the flour until a soft dough forms.
  4. Stir in the chocolate chips and scoop dough onto a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden around the edges.
